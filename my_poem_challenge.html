The future is incomplete without the past

In shadows cast by yesterday’s glow, We weave our dreams,
let hopes take flight, Each whisper of time, a gentle flow,
Guiding our visions through the night.

With every lesson, a stepping stone, The echoes of struggle
shape our way, For in the marrow of seeds we’ve sown,
Lies the promise of a brighter day.

We stand on cliffs, gazing ahead, The skyline beckons,
bold and vast, Yet in our hearts, the stories spread,
For the future is incomplete without the past.

Each triumph tasted, each tear we shed, A tapestry rich,
both thread and seam, In the quilt of life, our spirits are fed,
With every heartbeat, we dare to dream.

So let us honor the roads we’ve tread, Embrace the shadows,
the light amassed, For every step forward, the roots we spread
The future is incomplete without the past!!
