All about rabbits

basic info

Rabbits are little creatures with long ears and puffy tails, and they move their nose up and
down in an adorable way. they eat most orange vegetables in our world, and they
reproduce more than any human ever has

why rabbits make great pets?

  • they are cute and furry
  • they are often good listeners
  • they will eat all your left over carrots
